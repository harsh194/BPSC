üìà Significance of Muhammad Shah's Reign (1719‚Äì1748)

His long reign witnessed the decline of Mughal political authority and the rise of regional powers

The empire became more symbolic than real, setting the stage for Maratha expansion and British growth

These developments paved the way for British ascendancy in India
